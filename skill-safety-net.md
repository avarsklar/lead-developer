**The fear it kills:** *"If I touch it, I'll lose my work or my data."*

## What it does

- A **saved version** you can return to in one move.
- Your **secret keys** pulled out of the code and replaced with brand-new ones — so the exposed old ones are dead and useless.
- **Automatic backups** of your data.
- A **restore you watch work** with your own eyes — because a backup you've never restored from isn't really a backup.

After this, you literally cannot lose your work and you cannot lose your data.

## When to run it

Once per project, before your first real change goes live.

## When to stop or skip

It's a **one-time** setup — don't re-walk finished steps. A plain static site can skip backups (its history *is* the backup).

## Before this, you need

Nothing — this is the floor everything else rests on.

## After this — do this skill

**[`/ship-change`](skill-ship-change.html)** — make your first change the safe way. That completes Gate 1, and it's the loop you'll live in from here on.
