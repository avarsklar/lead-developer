**The fear it kills:** *"What if I break it and real users see it?"*

## What it does

- Sets your live app aside and **tries the change on a private copy** first.
- Lets you **preview and eyeball it** — nothing is live yet.
- **Publishes only on your okay**, with a fresh one-move way back every time.

## When to run it

**Every single time** you change anything — a button color, a typo, a whole feature. It doesn't matter how small.

## When to stop or skip

Never. No change is "too small for the loop" — a tiny change just runs it fast. This is the loop you live in from Gate 1 onward.

## Before this, you need

[`/safety-net`](skill-safety-net.html) — the floor underneath the loop.

## After this — do this skill

That's **Gate 1** — for most people, the whole emergency handled. Only if real money, other people's data, or people depending on uptime is in play do you climb to Gate 2: **[`/qa-harness`](skill-qa-harness.html)**.
